Usage: suspend [s1|s3]

Power states:
  s1: (referred to standby mode):
      Power on Suspend (POS): All the processor caches are flushed, and the CPU(s) stops executing instructions.
      The power to the CPU(s) and RAM is maintained. Devices that do not indicate they must remain on, may be powered off.
  s3: (referred to dram self-refresh mode):
      Suspend to RAM (STR): RAM remains powered.
